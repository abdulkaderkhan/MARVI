Watershed Interpolation Through Kriging Process

The start of each season was kriged from 2012 to 2018 and then compared. 2019 was excluded as there were significantly more data entries missing during this year.

The first season kriged was Winter, which starts in December and ends in February. Looking at the data, it is clear that 2016 has significantly higher groundwater levels as well as subsequent years when compared to pre-2016 levels.


The next season kriged was summer or pre-monsoon season, which begins in March and ends around May. From looking at our kriged data, it is noticeably apparent that more groundwater is available almost every subsequent year.
